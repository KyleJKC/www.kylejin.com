So I was thinking about the thing we were talking about earlier, and I cannot stop my self to write something.



Remember what happened after global studies discussion? Of course you do, that wasn’t a long time ago. But Tbh I don’t remember too much because I was so nervous and my brain wasn’t working. All I remember is I said “I liked u for a long time”, and I heard “I have the same feeling on u.” When I realized it was u the person who spoke this sentence, my feeling was indescribable. I had a crush on u for basically the whole school year as I told u on that day, and it seems like u are becoming my girlfriend, my first girlfriend in my life. That was such an amazing thing.



You took ur physics test on Tuesday. Before ur test, u asked me if I can help you to review physics stuff. I was still kinda nervous on Tuesday so I didn’t really answer ur every single question clearly. I gave u my graphing calculator before ur test but after that, I soon realized I didn’t tell you how to use that calculator to make graphs. After u gave me my calculator back, I turned it on and guilty came up immediately. What u typed in the graphing section wasn’t right. I was blaming myself for forgetting to tell you about how to type x and y stuff into calculator.



That Wednesday, that’s probably the day I enjoyed the most since I went to the states. I finished all my SSAs, and summer break was coming up, and I got to see my family. However, these didn’t actually make the day the happiest day of my life. What really made that day was, that afternoon, we were meeting for the last time for the first school year. I remember I was sitting on the grassland near kindergarten with you. We were talking about every single thing we came up with. I really enjoyed that afternoon I spent with you. We forgot the time until a bunch of kindergartners came to the field and started playing games and made us leave there. After that, you went get your art work and say goodbye to ur other friends, and lastly, I hugged u for the first time before u went home, I really wish that weren’t the only time we hugging each other.



After summer break began, I quickly found my judgment of u was absolutely right. U r such a nice and smart person and I found lots of our opinions on things and habits are pretty much the same.



Today u said we should just be friends for now since the distance between us is too long for us to really know each other well and do serious things. And without that dating is impossible. What u said definitely makes sense. I agree with that, it’s just in the meantime I feel a pity. U said u are not sure how will u feel next year. However, I know that could just be a smart way to make me feel not that bad.



Of course, I cannot blame anyone but me. After an afternoon thinking about this, I realized I should at least reach out and talk to u more often and build our friendship prior to the day I confessed to u. Without that, the relationship could be weak and easy to break, because we originally didn’t know each other very well and we didn’t have a strong friendship behind us to support our relationship.



Alright, I’m reading this after a few hours and I'm not sure **WHAT THE HECK** I was writing up there. So put all those random, corny and weird stuff and meaningless self blames away. From now on we are friends, like u said. What will happen in the future we leave this question to time to answer. no matter what will happen next year, no matter whether you will be willing to promote our friendship to the next level or not, I will always be willing to be your good friend and do my best to help you with whatever you want at any time.



6/28/23

**Dedicate to my friend and the best girl I’ve ever met —— Kathryn Brown**
